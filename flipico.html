Flipico

Picture puzzle game

We are all familiar with jigsaw puzzle games, and know what to do with those tiny strangely shaped pieces. But Flipico brings new dimension to picture puzzles. Puzzles vary from basic to really difficult ones, and principle is different. Instead of separate pieces, parts of the image are now flipped either vertically or horizontally, and your objective is to restore original image by flipping them back. And its not always easy to determine in which order those flips happened.
