Introduction

Komante is about software. Programming, new exciting applications and approaches, small web games, along with thoughts about all that. Its goal is to present original software products made by small independent teams and individuals, and to connect them in collaborative and constructive ways, ideas exchange, and business undertakings.

It offers overview of already developed products. All of them are free to use. Versatile technologies are included in their development, and cover different users and purposes. That's why products are divided into three categories. Apps for desktop applications, Tools for components, libraries and programming tools, and Games for html5 and javascript based web games.
