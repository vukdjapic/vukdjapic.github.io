About us

Concept

The main idea behind Komante is to bring to public some interesting applications, tools, pilot projects, programming thoughts, articles and ideas. And through them to present their authors. Their expertise, skills, ideas and professional attitude.

It is conceived as loose network of independent software developers and complement experts in IT industry, who can exchange ideas and expertise, and work together to provide professional services and create new applications.

Who we are

Komante is started by Vuk Djapic, computer software professional from Serbia. Worked as developer, senior developer and team leader in more than five companies, with dozen of clients. Experienced in web and server applications, software architecture and planning, rapid development. Started many independent projects, always aspiring to bring to life interesting ideas.
